NEXARA LABS — COMPANY OVERVIEW
==============================

Founded: 2018
Headquarters: Austin, Texas, USA
Stage: Series C (closed Q3 2023, $85M raised)
Employees: ~340 (as of Q1 2026)

FOUNDING STORY
--------------
Nexara Labs was founded in 2018 by Dr. Priya Anand (CEO) and Marcus Ellison (CTO), both
former research scientists at a large enterprise software company. Frustrated by the gap
between academic AI research and practical enterprise deployment, they set out to build a
platform that lets mid-market companies operationalise AI without requiring dedicated ML
engineering teams. The name "Nexara" derives from "nexus" and "ara" (Latin: altar) —
symbolising the firm's mission to be the connective hub where data, intelligence, and
business decisions meet.

MISSION
-------
Nexara Labs' stated mission is: "To make decision-grade AI accessible to every operations
team, not just the Fortune 500." The company focuses on lowering the barrier to enterprise
AI adoption through pre-built agent templates, a low-code workflow editor, and an
opinionated data integration layer that connects to existing ERP and CRM systems out of
the box.

PRODUCT LINES
-------------
1. NexaCore Platform
   The flagship SaaS product. A multi-tenant AI workflow platform that lets business
   analysts configure, deploy, and monitor AI-powered decision pipelines using a visual
   interface. Agents within NexaCore can call external APIs, query internal databases,
   and generate structured outputs that feed downstream business systems. Currently serves
   230 enterprise clients across manufacturing, logistics, and financial services.

2. NexaInsight
   An embedded analytics layer that sits on top of NexaCore, providing real-time dashboards
   of agent activity, model drift alerts, and cost attribution per workflow. Launched in
   Q2 2024. Adopted by 60% of existing NexaCore clients within 12 months of launch.

3. NexaGuard
   A compliance and AI governance module introduced in Q4 2024 in response to emerging EU
   AI Act requirements. Provides audit logs, bias scoring, and policy enforcement gates for
   AI workflows. Currently in pilot with 18 enterprise clients in the financial services
   sector.

4. Professional Services
   A small but growing consulting arm (~40 staff) that helps clients configure NexaCore for
   industry-specific use cases. Revenue from this segment is intentionally capped to keep
   the company's margins software-oriented.

CULTURE & VALUES
----------------
Nexara Labs operates under four stated values: Transparency (all model decisions must be
explainable to end users), Reliability (99.9% SLA on all production pipelines), Frugality
(efficient use of compute resources is a first-class engineering concern), and Inclusion
(product teams include at least one domain expert from the target industry during design).
